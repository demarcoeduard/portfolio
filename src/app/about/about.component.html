About

Hey there! I'm Eduard, but you can call me Edi. I'm a front-end developer based in Romania, with over a year of hands-on experience in crafting optimized and responsive websites to deliver the best user experience possible. I honed my skills through a front-end development bootcamp, which not only improved my technical abilities but also equipped me with valuable life skills.


During the bootcamp, I focused on creating user-friendly interfaces, writing clean and concise code, and adopting a problem-solving mindset. Additionally, collaborating with my professor and peers taught me essential communication skills, such as expressing my ideas in a concise and clear manner, active listening, and effective teamwork.


Overall, I'm passionate about creating front-end experiences and continuously refining my skills to deliver my best in every project.

Work Process

To better explain this process I'll use one of my recent projects as a example: my Hospital CRM. This project consists of three different stages: Researching, Designing, and Creating. Rather than diving straight into coding, I initiated the process by conduction a online research to gain a comprehensive understanding of what a CRM means, what are its essential components and how to tailor them for hospial use.


Next came the Designing phase, where I crafted all necessary elements such as headers, footers, and cards, ensuring they were not only aesthetically pleasing but also accessible and fully responsive. However, the most significant part is the last one where I add the all the necessary code to create a fully functional and optimized website for a flawless interactive user experience.


But I didn't mention an important part, which is when my cat, Afi, comes into the picture trying (and allways succeding) to get my attention. I mentioned Afi because he prevents me from experiencing burnout after long coding sessions, and he deserves recognition.
